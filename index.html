Yuxin LI

I'm currently a Principal Research Scientist at Desay SV in Singapore, where I lead research on end-to-end perception methods and generative methods for autonomous driving.

I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, under the supervision of Professor Yeo Chai Kiat.

Prior to that, I worked for 5 years as a Research Engineer in the fields of machine translation and video analysis.

I'm generally interested in efficient and robust methods for realistic low-latency solutions on edge devices.

Research

I'm interested in world models, new view synthesis, multi-task learning and system pruning. Most of my work focuses on simplifying complex systems to make them more robust and efficient.
